About TechFest 2025

Welcome to TechFest 2025

TechFest is the biggest annual technology event at our college, bringing together tech enthusiasts, innovators, and industry leaders.

From cutting-edge workshops to thrilling hackathons, TechFest is the perfect platform to **learn, compete, and showcase** your skills.

We aim to **push the boundaries of innovation** and create an environment where students can **explore new technologies** and build meaningful connections.
